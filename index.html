About

I am a Ph.D. student in Engineering Thermophysics at Beihang University, where I work on thermal management system and supercritical fluid heat transfer under the the supervision of Prof. Guoqiang Xu and Prof. Yanchen Fu. I am expected to obtain my Ph.D. degree in June 2026 and am currently seeking a postdoctoral position. If you are interested to chat with me, feel free to drop me an email.

My research focuses on Thermal Management Systems for aero engines, spanning multiscale topics from supercritical fluid heat transfer to advanced heat exchanger design and system-level thermal management. Currently I study thermal management for hydrogen-fueled aero engines.
